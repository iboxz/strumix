Strutile C10

Ceramic & Tile Adhesive for Use in Floor & Wall

Strutile C10 is a high quality adhesive for the permanent fixing of ceramics and tiles in floors or walls.

Download product catalog

Uses

  • Dry areas - rooms, corridors & halls
  • Wet areas - kitchens, WC and bathrooms when modified with Strubond Acro7
  • Fixing ceramics on floors

Advantages

  • Can be used in floors
  • Good open wet time
  • Good slip resistance
  • High adhesive bond strength
  • Water resistant
  • Can be used in totally submerged conditions

Standards compliance

Strutile C10 conforms with EN 12004, Class C, Type 1.

Properties

Appearance

White & Gray Powder

Specific gravity

Typically 1.5 gr/cm3 @ 20°C

Open time (25°c)

Approx. 20 min

Tensile adhesive strength

≥ 0.7 N/mm2

Instructions for use

  • Surface preparation: All surfaces must be sound and thoroughly clean before Strutile C10 is applied.
  • Mixing: Measure out 5 litres of clean water into the mixing vessel.
  • Application: Once mixed, Strutile C10 is typically spread on the substrate to a uniform thickness of 2 – 6 mm.

Packaging & Storage

  • Strutile C10 is available in 20 kg bags.
  • Minimum shelf life of 12 months at 20°C if kept in a dry store.

Health and safety

Strutile C10 is alkaline and should not come into contact with skin and eyes.

Fire

Strutile C10 is non-flammable.
