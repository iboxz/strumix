Strumould SLV

Chemical mould release agent

Strumould SLV is a chemical mould release agent specifically designed for quick, clean, and easy stripping of moulds and formwork. It ensures high quality, fair-faced, and stain-free concrete.

Download product catalog

Uses

  • For quick, clean, and easy stripping of moulds and formwork.
  • For high quality, fair-faced, and stain-free concrete.

Advantages

  • Ensures easy release, reducing damage and improving efficiency of production.
  • Minimizes surface blemishes and staining, promoting high-quality fair-faced concrete even with white cement.
  • Reduces opportunity for damage to formwork, extending usable life.

Additional Information

Strumould SLV inhibits corrosion of steel formwork and minimizes cleaning of formwork before re-use.

Properties

Appearance

Brown Liquid

Specific gravity

Typically 0.84±0.02 at 20°C

Storage

Strumould SLV has a minimum shelf life of 12 months when stored in the original unopened containers under normal warehouse conditions.

Packaging

Available in 20 Litre containers and 208 Litre drums.

Instructions for Use

  • Clean all moulds thoroughly to remove rust, concrete, oil, or other materials before use.
  • New timber moulds can be coated directly with Strumould SLV; for regular reuse, coat with a suitable polyurethane sealer first.
  • Apply using normal methods such as brush, sponge, or spray.

Health and Safety

Strumould SLV does not fall into hazard classifications, but suitable protective gloves and goggles should be worn. It should not be swallowed or come into contact with skin and eyes.

Fire

Strumould SLV is flammable.
