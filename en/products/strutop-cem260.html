Strutop Cem260

Non-Film Forming and polishing Water Repellent Sealer

Strutop Cem260 is a single component penetrating siliconate system which penetrates into porous substrates and then reacts to produce a bonded hydrophobic lining to the pores.

Download product catalog

Uses

  • To protect atmospherically exposed reinforced concrete structures from attack by chloride ions and water intrusion.
  • Suitable for new and existing structures, including those in coastal environments.
  • Particularly suitable for decorative, stained or exposed-aggregate concrete and for natural stone, bricks and pavers.

Advantages

  • Penetrates into substrates
  • Non staining
  • Polishing
  • Reduces water and chloride intrusion
  • Increases freeze thaw resistance
  • Minimizes efflorescence
  • Allows water vapour to escape from the structure
  • Chemically resistant to ice melting compounds, fuels, oils and atmospheric contaminants

Additional Information

Strutop Cem260 does not discolour most substrates, but it shines and has excellent resistance to weathering.

Properties

Appearance

Colorless or yellowish liquid

Specific Gravity

1.10 ± 0.02 gr/cm3 @ 20°C

Number of coat

2

Theoretical application rate per coat

0.2 kg/m2

Instructions for use

  • All surfaces should be dry and free from contamination.
  • Apply in two flood coats until the recommended total application rate has been achieved.
  • Allow to dry for a minimum of 2 hours (@ 20ºC) before continuing.

Packaging & Storage

  • Available in 20 kg containers.
  • Shelf life of 12 months when stored in cool, dry conditions.

Health and safety

Strutop Cem260 does not fall into the hazard classifications of current regulations. However, it should not be swallowed or allowed to come into contact with skin and eyes.

Fire

Strutop Cem260 is non-flammable.
