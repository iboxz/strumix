Strumix

  Stramix is a research-driven organization focused on delivering innovative products and services aimed at facilitating faster, easier, more cost-effective, and longer-lasting construction. Leveraging its expertise and experience in research and development, technical engineering, production, implementation, and consulting in building chemicals, concrete additives, waterstops, and spacers, the group continuously strives to enhance the quality of the construction industry using modern construction techniques.

Stramix collaborates directly with the Iranian Paint and Resin Research Institute in various fields, including waterproof surface coatings and resins used in concrete, and has been officially recognized by this institute for its commitment to research and innovation. Additionally, the group's management is conducted by its owners, ensuring streamlined processes and faster decision-making for clients. Stramix guarantees prompt product delivery, accurate and timely technical responses, and excellent after-sales service for its customers.
